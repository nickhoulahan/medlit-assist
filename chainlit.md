# Welcome to MedLit Assist 🏥

Your AI-powered biomedical research assistant that makes medical literature accessible to everyone.

## What I Can Do

- 🔍 **Search PubMed Central** for the latest biomedical research
- 📚 **Explain complex research** in simple, easy-to-understand language  
- 💡 **Answer your questions** about scientific articles with proper citations
- 🎯 **Synthesize findings** from multiple research papers
- 🗣️ **Translate medical jargon** into everyday language without losing accuracy

## How to Use

Simply ask me about any biomedical or health topic! For example:

- *"What's the latest research on diabetes treatment?"*
- *"Tell me about cancer immunotherapy"*
- *"How does aspirin work in the body?"*
- *"What do we know about Alzheimer's prevention?"*

I'll search PubMed Central, analyze the research, and explain the findings in a way that anyone can understand.

If you want to ask a follow up question about any of the articles, phrase your question like "Can you review the first article and tell me more about ...?"
